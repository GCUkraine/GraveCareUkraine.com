GraveCareUkraine provides grave care and cemetery maintenance services in Kyiv and across Ukraine, including:

  • Year-round care — seasonal visits throughout the year

  • Fresh flower placement

The exact scope of each order is always agreed with the customer before any work begins and before any payment is made.

For first-time customers, a prepayment is required before the visit. The remaining balance is settled after you receive the photo report confirming the work is complete. Returning customers are not required to prepay — this is built on trust.
