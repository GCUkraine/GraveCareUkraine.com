The process

How it works

We try to keep things simple and human. Here's what happens after you write to us.

First request This is how it works the first time. Returning families have a simpler flow — see note below.
01
Write to us
Send a message through the form below, or write on WhatsApp. Tell us the city, the cemetery and whatever you know about the grave location — even rough details are fine.
02
We get back to you
Within 24 hours, we'll reply with a clear price and timeline — or a question or two if we need more information to proceed.
03
A small prepayment
We ask for prepayment before the first visit — via PayPal, bank transfer, Western Union or another method convenient for you. USD, EUR and UAH all work.
04
We visit, you get photos
We complete the visit within 14 days. If weather gets in the way, we'll let you know and reschedule. You receive at least 4 before-and-after photos, then we settle the remaining balance.
Families who've worked with us before

After your first completed visit, we no longer ask for prepayment. It's built on trust, not paperwork.
