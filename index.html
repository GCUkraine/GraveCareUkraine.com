How it works

Simple, transparent and reliable. You're kept informed at every step.

01
Submit your request
Fill in the form, write to us on email-btn or WhatsApp. Tell us the cemetery and grave location — or just a name and city.
02
We confirm & quote
We respond within 24 hours with a confirmed price, timeline and any questions we need answered.
03
You pay securely
PayPal, bank transfer, Western Union or any convenient method. We accept USD, EUR and UAH.
04
Work is completed
Service carried out within 14 days. Weather permitting. We notify you when it's scheduled.
