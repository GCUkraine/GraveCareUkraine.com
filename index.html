The process

How it works

Simple, transparent and reliable. You are kept informed at every step.

01
Submit your request
Fill in the form or write to us on WhatsApp. Tell us the city, cemetery and grave location — or whatever you remember about the burial place.
02
We confirm & quote
We respond within 24 hours with a price and timeline, or with any questions we need answered to proceed.
03
Prepayment
Prepayment for the agreed services via PayPal, bank transfer, Western Union or any convenient method. We accept USD, EUR and UAH.
04
Work is completed
Service carried out within 14 days (usually 7), weather permitting. We notify you when it's scheduled.
05
Photo report sent
You receive minimum 4 photos before and 4 after, by email or messenger. No surprises — only results.
